About AfriGlam

AfriGlam combines traditional African textiles with contemporary designs to create wearable art. Our mission is to make heritage bold, modern, and accessible.

Founded in 2020 by fashion enthusiast Amina Okoro, AfriGlam started as a small tailoring shop in Lagos, Nigeria. Today, we collaborate with artisans across Africa to bring you unique pieces that celebrate culture and craftsmanship.

We believe in sustainable fashion, using eco-friendly materials and ethical production practices. Each piece is handcrafted with care, ensuring quality and authenticity.

Join us on our journey to celebrate African heritage through fashion. Explore our collections and experience the vibrant world of AfriGlam.
